Help Centre

📦 Orders & Shipping

How do I track my order? +
Go to the Delivery page and enter your order number to see real-time tracking updates.
What are the delivery options? +
We offer standard and express delivery across the Philippines. Delivery time depends on your location and order size.
Can I cancel or modify my order? +
Yes, you can cancel or modify your order within 24 hours after placing it. Contact our support team immediately for assistance.

💰 Payments & Refunds

What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept credit/debit cards, GCash, Maya, and cash on delivery.
How do I request a refund? +
Visit the Return & Refund page, fill out the form, and our support team will process your request within 3–5 business days.
